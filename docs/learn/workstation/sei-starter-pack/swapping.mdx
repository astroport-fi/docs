In this tutorial, you'll learn how to efficiently trade your assets on Sei using Astroport's intuitive swap interface. Whether you're a newcomer or an experienced trader, this tutorial ensures a straightforward and efficient trading experience.

### Step 1: Access Astroport and Link Your Wallet

Begin at [sei.astroport.fi/swap](https://sei.astroport.fi/swap). Press "Connect Wallet" to continue.

<img src={ Swapping1 } style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

A variety of wallet choices will appear for your selection. 

<img src={ Swapping2 } style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

### Step 2: Choose Your Swap Pair

Navigate to the dropdown menu to pick the token you'd like to swap and the token you aim to receive.

<img src={ Swapping3 } style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

### Step 3: Define Your Slippage Tolerance

Set your acceptable range of price variation by adjusting the slippage settings.

<img src={ Swapping4 } style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

### Step 4: Enter the Amount to Swap

Indicate the number of tokens you're swapping. When ready, press "Swap" to proceed.

<img src={ Swapping5 } style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

### Step 5: Review and Confirm Your Swap

Inspect your swap details, ensuring all looks well. Hit "Confirm Swap" to advance.

<img src={ Swapping6 } style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

### Step 6: Sign and Confirm Your Transaction

At this step, sign and confirm the transaction, offering the needed authorization to seal your token swap.

<img src={ Swapping7 } style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

### Step 7: Swap Accomplished!

Once the swap is successfully executed, a confirmation notification will be displayed on the swap page, verifying the completion of the transaction.

<img src={ Swapping8 } style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />
