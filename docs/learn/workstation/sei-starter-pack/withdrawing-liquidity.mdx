Taking control of your assets on Astroport is intuitive. In this tutorial, we'll guide you through the steps of withdrawing liquidity, ensuring you have a smooth and transparent experience from start to finish.

### Step 1: Access Astroport and Link Your Wallet

Begin at [sei.astroport.fi/pools](https://sei.astroport.fi/pools). Press "Connect Wallet" to continue.

<img src={ WithdrawingLiquidity1 } style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

A variety of wallet choices will appear for your selection.

<img src={ WithdrawingLiquidity2 } style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

### Step 2: Choose a Liquidity Pool to Manage

Identify and select the specific pool you're interested in. Once you've pinpointed it, click "Manage".

<img src={ WithdrawingLiquidity3 } style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

### Step 3: Navigate to the Withdraw Liquidity Page

Select the "withdraw liquidity" option to be directed to the dedicated page where you'll specify your withdrawal details.

<img src={ WithdrawingLiquidity4 } style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

### Step 4: Enter the Number of LP Tokens to Withdraw

Indicate the exact number of LP tokens you aim to withdraw. These LP tokens will be burned, and in exchange, you'll receive the tokens that make up the pair.

<img src={ WithdrawingLiquidity5 } style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

### Step 5: Initiate the Withdrawal

Having determined the desired withdrawal amount, click on "Withdraw Liquidity" to move forward.

<img src={ WithdrawingLiquidity6 } style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

### Step 6: Confirm Withdrawing Liquidity

A summary of your withdrawal will be presented. Review it and then select "Confirm Withdrawing Liquidity" to progress.

<img src={ WithdrawingLiquidity7 } style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

### Step 7: Sign and Confirm Your Transaction

At this step, sign and confirm the transaction, solidifying your withdrawal from the selected pool.

<img src={ WithdrawingLiquidity8 } style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

### Step 8: Withdrawal Completed!

Upon successful completion of the withdrawal, a confirmation notification will appear on your screen, indicating that the process was executed seamlessly.

<img src={ WithdrawingLiquidity9 } style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />
