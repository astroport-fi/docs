Venture into liquidity provision on Astroport, the leading DEX on Sei optimized for both XYK and stableswap pools. 

### Step 1: Access Astroport and Link Your Wallet

Begin at [sei.astroport.fi/pools](https://sei.astroport.fi/pools). Press "Connect Wallet" to continue.

<img src={ DepositingLiquidity1 } style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

A variety of wallet choices will appear for your selection.

<img src={ DepositingLiquidity2 } style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

### Step 2: Pool Selection

Browse through the available pools and select the one you wish to contribute liquidity to. Select "Add Liquidity" to continue.

<img src={ DepositingLiquidity3 } style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

### Step 3: Set Your Slippage Tolerance

Decide on the maximum acceptable price variation for your liquidity provision by tuning the slippage parameters.

<img src={ DepositingLiquidity5 } style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

### Step 4: Select an Amount to Provide

Choose the quantity of liquidity you're willing to provide. Utilize the drag bar for easy adjustments.

<img src={ DepositingLiquidity4 } style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

### Step 5: Initiate Liquidity Provision

Select "Add Liquidity" to progress.

<img src={ DepositingLiquidity6 } style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

### Step 6: Review and Confirm Liquidity Addition

Once content with your transaction details, click on "Confirm Providing Liquidity" to continue. 

<img src={ DepositingLiquidity7 } style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

### Step 7: Sign and Confirm Your Transaction

At this step, sign and confirm the transaction, solidifying your deposit to the selected pool.

<img src={ DepositingLiquidity8 } style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

### Step 8: Liquidity Provision Accomplished!

A confirmation notification will pop up on the page, verifying the successful provision of your liquidity.

<img src={ DepositingLiquidity9 } style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />
