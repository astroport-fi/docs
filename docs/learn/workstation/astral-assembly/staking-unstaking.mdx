## Staking ASTRO


### Step 1: Go to the Astroport Web App governance tab: https://app.astroport.fi/governance

<img src={Staking1Url} style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

Select the "Stake ASTRO" button to move forward.

### Step 2: Select the Amount of ASTRO to Stake

<img src={Staking2Url} style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

You can use the slider or click on the percentages to help you select an amount. 

### Step 3: Confirm Staking

<img src={Staking3Url} style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

Select "Confirm Staking" to move forward.

### Step 4: Sign and Broadcast Transaction!

<img src={Staking4Url} style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

You will be prompted to sign the transaction using your connected wallet. Once the transaction is confirmed, your ASTRO has been converted to xASTRO!

## Unstaking xASTRO

### Step 1: Go to the Astroport Web App governance tab: https://app.astroport.fi/governance

<img src={Staking5Url} style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

### Step 2: Select the Amount of xASTRO to Unstake

<img src={Staking6Url} style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

You can use the slider or click on the percentages to help you select an amount. 

### Step 3: Confirm Unstaking

<img src={Staking7Url} style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

Select "Confirm Unstaking" to move forward.

### Step 4: Sign and Broadcast Transaction!

<img src={Staking8Url} style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

You will be prompted to sign the transaction using your connected wallet. Once the transaction is confirmed, your xASTRO will be burned. Unstaking claims the initial staked amount + accrued ASTRO since staking.
