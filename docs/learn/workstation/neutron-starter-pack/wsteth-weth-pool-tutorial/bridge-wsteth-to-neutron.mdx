# Bridge wstETH to Neutron 

### Step 1: Head over to [bridge.neutron.org/bridge](https://bridge.neutron.org/bridge)

Now that we have wstETH on your Ethereum wallet, the next step is to bridge this to Neutron. We can do this using the [official Neutron bridge](https://bridge.neutron.org/bridge). 

Select "Connect Cosmos Wallet" on the top right to continue.  

<img src={Neutron1Url} style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

### Step 2: Connect Cosmos Wallet 

From the menu, select a supported wallet to connect.

<img src={Neutron2Url} style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

If this is your first time connecting, you will be prompted to approve the connection with your wallet. 

<img src={Neutron2BUrl} style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

### Step 3: Connect Ethereum Wallet

Once your Cosmos wallet is connected, we will want to do the same for our Ethereum wallet. Select "Connect Metamask" to continue. 

<img src={Neutron3Url} style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

You will be prompted to connect your Metamask wallet to continue. 

<img src={Neutron3BUrl} style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

### Step 4: Select Amount and Destination 

Once both wallets are connected, you can select the amount of wstETH to bridge and fill in the destination address. Select "Sign Transaction" to continue. 

<img src={Neutron4Url} style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

### Step 5: Sign and Confirm Transaction 

To complete the transaction, sign and confirm with your Metamask wallet. 

<img src={Neutron5Url} style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

### Step 6: Wait for Transaction to complete

After signing the transaction, you will be prompted that the transaction may take about 20 minutes to bridge. In the meantime, you can inspect your transaction using the provided link to the block explorer. 

<img src={Neutron6Url} style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

You will be prompted on your screen once the transaction is complete. 

<img src={Neutron6BUrl} style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />
