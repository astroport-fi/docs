# Swap wstETH for WETH on Astroport 

### Step 1: Head over to [neutron.astroport.fi/swap](https://neutron.astroport.fi/swap) and Connect a Wallet

Select "Connect Wallet" in the top right to continue.

<img src={AstroSwap1Url} style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

Select an availiable wallet from the list and connect. 

<img src={AstroSwap2Url} style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

### Step 2: Select Swap Route (wstETH to WETH)

The simplest way to jump into the wstETH - WETH pool on Astroport is to bridge wstETH using the Neutron bridge and then swap wstETH for WETH for the other half needed to provide liquidity. 

<Admonition type="info" title="NOTE">
    You can bridge over WETH yourself, however, bridging cost are typically high for this type of transaction and the wstETH - WETH pool on Astroport (currently above 2M) supports most trades with low slippage. If you are making larger trades, then the bridging fees may be worth it compared to the slippage loss. 
</Admonition>

Select the sell (wstETH) and buy (WETH) tokens in the drop down menus to continue. **Note that the Wrapped version of ETH we are swapping into is wrapped using the Axelar bridge.**

<img src={AstroSwap3Url} style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

### Step 3: Select an Amount to Swap 

To get a 50/50 ratio between your wstETH and WETH holdings, you can select the 50% option. Select "Swap" to continue. 

<img src={AstroSwap4Url} style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

### Step 4: Confirm and Sign Transaction 

Next, you will be prompted to confirm your transaction. 

<img src={AstroSwap5Url} style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

Sign the transaction using your wallet to continue. 

<img src={AstroSwap5BUrl} style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

You will be prompted with a notification on your screen once your transaction is complete. 

<img src={AstroSwap5CUrl} style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />
