# Unstaking/Withdrawing Liquidity

### Step 1: Head over to [neutron.astroport.fi/pools](https://neutron.astroport.fi/pools)

To withdraw our liquidity, we will first need to unstake our LP tokens. Select the Manage tab and "Unstake" to continue. 

<img src={Withdraw1Url} style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

### Step 2: Select Amount of LP Tokens to Unstake 

Select "Unstake LP Tokens" to continue. 

<img src={Withdraw2Url} style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

### Step 3: Confirm and Sign Transaction

You will be prompted to confirm the transaction. Select "Confirm Unstaking LP Tokens" to continue. 

<img src={Withdraw3Url} style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

Sign the transaction using your wallet to continue. 

<img src={Withdraw4Url} style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

You will be prompted with a notification on your screen once your LP tokens have been unstaked. You can now continue to withdrawing your LP tokens. 

<img src={Withdraw5Url} style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

### Step 4: Withdraw LP tokens 

Head back to the pools page, select "Manage" and "Withdraw Liquidity" to withdraw your LP tokens. 

<img src={Withdraw6Url} style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

### Step 5: Select an Amount to Withdraw

Select "Withdraw Liquidity" to continue. 

<img src={Withdraw7Url} style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

### Step 6: Confirm and Sign Transaction

You will be prompted to confirm the transaction. Select "Confirm Withdrawing Liquidity" to continue. 

<img src={Withdraw8Url} style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

Sign the transaction using your wallet to continue. 

<img src={Withdraw9Url} style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />

You will be prompted with a notification on your screen once your liquidity has been withdrawn.

<img src={Withdraw10Url} style={{ paddingBottom: 10 }} />
